# DevCard - The NFC Business Card

Welcome to DevCard, the ultimate business card solution for electronics engineers and hardware tinkerers. Designed with the modern professional in mind, DevCard leverages the power of NFC technology to create a memorable and interactive networking experience.

## Features

- **NFC Functionality**: Powered by the NT3H2211W0FTTJ chip, DevCard offers seamless interaction with smartphones and NFC readers, instantly connecting to your LinkedIn profile.
- **Custom NFC Antenna**: Showcasing superior layout and design skills, the card includes a meticulously designed NFC antenna, optimized for the best performance within the standard credit card dimensions.
- **Energy Harvesting**: Utilizing the NFC field's energy, DevCard lights up an LED, demonstrating an innovative application of energy harvesting technology.
- **Compact Design**: With the same dimensions as a credit card, DevCard fits perfectly in any wallet, ensuring you're always ready to make a professional impression.

## Getting Started

To experience DevCard, simply hold an NFC-enabled device close to the card. The device will detect the card and prompt you to open a link to the LinkedIn profile associated with it. No additional software or app installation is required.
